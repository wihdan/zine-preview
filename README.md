# Zine Preview

A simple web application to turn your flat image spreads into a realistic, flippable digital zine. Perfect for designer, creator or zine makers who want to preview their layouts.

## Features

- Upload & Arrange: Drag and drop your pages (A5 size). If you upload a landscape spread, it automatically splits into two A5 pages.
- 3D Preview: Flip through your zine with realistic page turns, spine shadows, and paper textures.
- Export: Save your spread layouts as PNG mockups with customizable backgrounds.
